I am an Associate Editor for Physical Review Letters , the flagship journal published by the American Physical Society. My primary responsibility is to oversee the peer review process and decide which papers merit publication according to the journal's criteria.

My research interests are primarliy in high energy QCD and the phenomenology of ultra-relativistic heavy-ion collisions. I received my Ph.D. from Stomy Brook University in 2008 following which I held postdoctoral research positions in the nuclear theory groups at Brookhaven National Laboratory and North Carolina State University. I began studying physics while a Mechanical Engineering student at The Cooper Union.
