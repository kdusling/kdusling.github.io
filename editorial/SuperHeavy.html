On June 8th, 2016 the IUPAC has announced the names for four new elements with atomic numbers Z=113, 115, 117, 118.

A review of the claims for discovery of Z=113, 115 and 117 can be found in Pure Appl. Chem. 88 (2016) 139, and for Z=118 in Pure Appl. Chem. 88 (2016) 155 .
