Quadratic Formula
 $ax^2+bx+c=0\qquad x=\dfrac{-b\pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$
Power Rule
 $x=t^n \quad\text{then}\quad \dot{x}=n t^{n-1}$
Velocity and Acceleration in Rectilinear Motion
 $v=\dfrac{dx}{dt}\qquad a=\dfrac{dv}{dt}\qquad a=\dfrac{d^2x}{dt^2}\qquad a=v\dfrac{dv}{dx}$
Uniformaly accelerated rectilinear motion
 $v=v_0+at$
$x=x_0+v_0t +\dfrac{1}{2}at^2$
$v^2=v_0^2+2a\left(x-x_0\right)$
Tangential and Normal Components
 $\vec{v}=v \hat{e_t}$
$\vec{a}=\dfrac{dv}{dt}\hat{e_t}+\dfrac{v^2}{\rho}\hat{e_n}$
Radial and Transverse Components
 $\vec{v}=\dot{r} \hat{e_r} + r\dot{\theta}\hat{e_\theta}$
$\vec{a}=\left(\ddot{r}-r\dot{\theta}^2\right)\hat{e_r} + \left(r\ddot{\theta}+2\dot{r}\dot{\theta}\right)\hat{e_\theta}$
Newton's second law
 $\sum F_x=ma_x \qquad \sum F_y=ma_y$
Newton's second law
using tangential and normal components
 $\sum F_t=m\dfrac{dv}{dt} \qquad \sum F_n=m\dfrac{v^2}{\rho}$
Newton's second law
using radial and transverse components
 $\sum F_r=m\left(\ddot{r}-r\dot{\theta}^2\right) \qquad \sum F_\theta=m\left(r\ddot{\theta}+2\dot{r}\dot{\theta}\right)$
Angular Momentum
 $\vert{\vec H}_0\vert=H_0=rmv\sin\phi$
Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation
 $F=G\dfrac{Mm}{r^2}\qquad G=\begin{cases}6.67\times 10^{-11}\frac{\text{N m}^2}{\text{kg}^2} \\3.44\times 10^{-8} \frac{\text{lb ft}^2}{\text{slug}^2}\end{cases}$
