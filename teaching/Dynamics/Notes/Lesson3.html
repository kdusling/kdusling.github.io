Newton's 2nd Law

The statement of Newton's 2nd Law is as follows. A particle has an acceleration proportional to the resultant force acting on it. The direction of the acceleration is the same as the direction of the resultant force. The proportionality constant is the mass of the particle.

Newton's 2nd Law
\begin{align*} \vec{F}=m\vec{a} \end{align*}
Newton's 2nd Law with multiple forces
\begin{align*} \sum \vec{F}=m\vec{a} \end{align*}
