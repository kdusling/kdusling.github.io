Sample Problem 1-4
In the pulley system shown below mass \(A\) has a velocity of 2 ft/s at some instant in time. What is the upward speed of mass \(B\) at the same instant?
Let us call the total length of the rope \(L\). \begin{align*} L=x_A + 4x_B \end{align*} Taking the time derivative (noting that \(L\) is a constant) \begin{align*} 0&=v_A + 4v_B\\ v_B &= -\frac{1}{4}v_A\\ v_B &= -\frac{1}{4}(2~\textrm{ft/s})\\ v_B &= -0.5~\textrm{ft/s} \end{align*} So, the velocity of mass \(B\) is 0.5 ft/s upward.
