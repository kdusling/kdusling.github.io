Princeton's SuperPipe facility has studied turbulent flow over a very large range of Reynolds numbers in smooth circular pipes. The SuperPipe data can be found at the Princeton's Gas Dynamics Laboratory website.

Some recent physics papers that address turbulent friction in rough pipes, and the relation to Kolmogorov theory and critical phenomena include:

M. Mehrafarin and N. Pourtolami, Phys. Rev. E 77, 055304 (2008)
Intermittency and rough-pipe turbulence

N. Goldenfeld, Phys. Rev. Lett. 96, 044503 (2006)
Roughness-Induced Critical Phenomena in a Turbulent Flow
